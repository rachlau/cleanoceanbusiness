About Us

Our Mission is to Promote Businesses that Protect the Marine Animals and the Bay

Clean Ocean Business aims to provide small businesses a platform to promote their business as well as creating awarness for businesses that helps protect our environment.

Who We Are

CleanOceanBusiness.com is a (local non-profit) partner for the City of Santa Cruz Environmental Compliance Program dedicated to supporting businesses making a positive difference in maintaining a healthy environment and protecting our waterways.

Our Vision

To help people - help business - help the environment. To offer an online local business platform for companies who are recognized for their excellence, earning the Clean Ocean Business Award. Our mission is to help our businesses and environment thrive. To offer our community online access to easily find, explore, learn about and support Clean Ocean Businesses in their neighborhoods. Clean Ocean Businesses protect the Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuary through practices that keep pollutants away from both storm drains and the sewer system.

Our Mission

To offer Our Community – an easy-to-use, enjoyable, online portal – featuring a complete directory of “Clean Ocean Businesses” searchable by neighborhoods and business type; providing up-to-date contact information, photos, articles, reviews, posts and promotions

Why Choose Us?

Clean Ocean Business promotes marine life and the protection of the Ocean.

Sustainability

Many businesses in Santa Cruz have been protecting the environment through their use of sustainable materials.

Wide Range Of Small Businesses

There are many businesses such as Restaurants and Vehicle services that aim to protect marine life and the bay.

Promoting small businesses

There are many small business to choose from when using Clean Ocean Business to choose your next location to eat or service your vehicle.

Our History

The Clean Ocean Business Program was started by the City of Santa Cruz, Environmental Compliance Department to recognize businesses that are in compliance with the City's mandatory BMPs (Best Management Practices) by reducing water pollution from auto repair shops, other types of vehicle service facilities, and food service facilities. The award-winning efforts of these businesses have protected the San Lorenzo River, the Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuary and local creeks for over 20 years through practices that keep pollutants away from both storm drains and the sewer system.

Our Growth

The City of Santa Cruz now recognizes over 300 Clean Ocean Business awardees and 48 have been recipients since the City program began 21 years ago. In 2022 CleanOceanBusinsess.com, created an online community business platform to actively support, promote and advertise businesses known for their excellence and who have earned the “Clean Ocean Business Award”.

Ultimate Goal

To continue offering a welcoming, supportive, engaging online platform for Clean Ocean Businesses and our Communities to interface, grow business, grow community and find each other. To help expand the City of Santa Cruz’s Environmental Compliance Program and Award Certification Status.
