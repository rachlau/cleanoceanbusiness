Frequently Asked Questions

We are dedicated to supporting businesses that are making a positive difference in maintaining and protecting our ocean, rivers, waterways and eco-system. We are here to help businesses thrive by offering certified Clean Ocean Business an online platform to advertise their business, services and products.

Clean Ocean Business is a non-profit, tax-exempt 501(c)(3) Donations are tax-deductible as allowed by law.

A membership lets you create a secure platform that allows you to deliver content easily and automatically. It makes it easier to prevent outsiders from stealing and misusing your content. With a membership, you can get passive income from your content and give value to customers.

About Site

CleanOceanBusiness.com is a (local non-profit) partner for the City of Santa Cruz Environmental Compliance Program dedicated to supporting businesses making a positive difference in maintaining a healthy environment and protecting our waterways.
